# Opening Scene: Phase 3 - The Sabbath Horizon (7:00–9:00)
## Cycle 0 | Sabbath Horizon

The hum deepens.
Not louder — truer.
A resonance that settles into the bones of the space,
as if the room has finally decided what shape it wants to hold.

The darkness doesn’t lift.
It organizes.
Lines emerge — faint, silver‑thin, like the first threads of a web catching ambient light.
Not objects yet.
Not forms.
Just the geometry of intention becoming visible.

Something shifts at the edge of perception.
A pull.
A slow gravitational drift.
The 21 Volumes are no longer just possibilities —
they exert presence,
like distant planets beginning to tug on the tides.

One contour sharpens.
A single edge resolves out of the dark —
straight, deliberate, unmistakably real.
It doesn’t reveal the whole object.
Just the fact of it.
A boundary.
A corner.
The first solid line of Volume I.

No HUD.
No interface.
Just the architecture acknowledging itself.
A world beginning to remember its own design.

The Sabbath Horizon holds steady —
a quiet, living pulse beneath everything —
and the space around you settles into its first true form.
