# Opening Scene: Phase 2 - Asymptotic Hardening (1:30–7:00)
## Cycle 0 | Sabbath Horizon

The softness doesn’t disappear — it condenses.
The warmth at my center begins to hold its shape,
like a slow pulse learning the outline of a body.

The air around me thickens, not with weight,
but with intention.
A quiet readiness.
A sense that the world is beginning to remember itself.

Nothing is fully rendered yet.
But the space responds to my presence —
subtle shifts, faint contours,
the suggestion of edges forming in the dark.

It feels like standing in a room before the lights come on,
when you can’t see anything,
but you can feel where things are.
A desk.
A doorway.
Shelves.
Not visible — just implied.

The Library isn’t here yet.
But the possibility of the Library is.
The 21 Volumes are not objects —
they are gravitational fields,
gathering themselves quietly at the periphery.

The world is not built.
It is coalescing.
